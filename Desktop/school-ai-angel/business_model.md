# Angels AI School - Zero-Cost Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Vision**: Build the largest free school management platform in Africa, then monetize through indirect revenue streams that don't burden schools.

**Three-Tier Product Strategy**:
1. **Core** (Offline) - 200MB, >1GB RAM, 100% offline
2. **Hybrid** (Smart Sync) - 50MB, 512MB RAM, on-device AI + cloud sync

**Revenue Goal**: $0 cost to 95%+ of schools, monetize through alternative channels to reach profitability.

## Cloud Storage Economics (Supabase Free Tier)

### The 500MB Problem

**Supabase free tier**: 500MB storage per project

**Your platform** (per school):
- Student records: ~5KB each Ã— 500 students = 2.5MB
- AI results cache: ~100KB/day Ã— 30 days = 3MB
- Messages/notifications: ~50KB/day Ã— 30 days = 1.5MB
- Total per school: ~7MB/month

**Capacity**: 500MB Ã· 7MB = ~70 schools per free project

**Solutions**:

#### Option 1: Multi-Tenancy Optimization
- Aggressive data compression
- Prune old AI results (>90 days)
- Store media externally (Cloudflare R2 free tier)
- **Result**: 200-300 schools per free project

#### Option 2: School Federation Model
- Each school gets own Supabase project (free)
- They own their data
- You provide central coordination
- **Result**: Unlimited schools, $0 cost

#### Option 3: Growth-Triggered Migration
- Start free tier
- At 80% capacity, apply for:
  - NGO grant ($50K)

  - Sponsor funding
- **Result**: Pay for Supabase Pro ($25/month) once revenue secured
